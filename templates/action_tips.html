In the Action phase, the objective is to execute specific actions on the compromised system to achieve the attacker's goals, such as data theft, system manipulation, or further compromise.

A real attacker may aim to steal files, capture keystrokes, or intercept sensitive network information.
They can also attempt to move from one infrastructure to another in order to expand their control and infect other systems within the network.

In essence, in this phase, the attacker can do or take whatever they want.
